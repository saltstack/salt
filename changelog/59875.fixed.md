Added a clarifying note to the ``master_job_cache`` figure caption
in ``doc/topics/jobs/external_cache.rst`` explaining that not every
returner satisfies the master-job-cache contract; one-way sinks like
``syslog`` are valid minion-side returners but cannot be used as a
master job cache because they do not implement ``save_load`` /
``get_load`` / ``prep_jid``.
