Fixed ``pkg.group_installed``/``pkg.group_info`` failing to expand a dnf environment group whose member groups have multi-word names (e.g. ``Group '@Common NetworkManager submodules' not found`` when installing ``Workstation`` on RHEL/AlmaLinux 8, 9 and 10). The member group is now resolved by its bare name when the ``@``-prefixed lookup fails. This affects dnf4 only; dnf5 group handling is unchanged.
