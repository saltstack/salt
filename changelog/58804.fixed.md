Documented the recovery flow for a lost minion key (container
reinstall, missing ``pki_dir``) in the new
``doc/security/key-lifecycle.rst`` page: the operator must delete
the old accepted key with ``salt-key -d <id>`` and verify the new
fingerprint out of band before accepting; skipping either step
either deadlocks the new minion in ``minions_denied/`` or trusts an
unverified key.
