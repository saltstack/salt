Fixed ``saltutil.runner``/``saltutil.wheel`` failing git-backed master functions (e.g. ``git_pillar.update``) with ``failed to stat '/root/.gitconfig'`` when the master runs as a non-root user. Dropping to the master user with ``chugid`` left ``HOME``/``USER``/``LOGNAME`` pointing at the invoking (root) user; these are now aligned with the runas user, and pygit2's cached global-config search path is refreshed.
