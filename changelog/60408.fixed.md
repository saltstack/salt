Documented the ``s3.location``, ``s3.service_url``, ``s3.https_enable``, ``s3.path_style``, and ``s3.verify_ssl`` master config options in the ``s3fs`` fileserver module docstring. The new "Regional endpoints" section explains why s3fs may fail with ``No AWSAccessKey was presented`` or a SigV4 region-mismatch error against buckets outside ``us-east-1`` and what setting to use to fix it. A test in ``tests/pytests/unit/fileserver/test_s3fs_documented_options.py`` pins the option names to the loader so the docs cannot silently drift.
